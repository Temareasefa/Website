Information Technology

Information Technology (IT) refers to the use of computers, software, networks, and other technologies to manage, process, store, and disseminate information. It encompasses a broad range of activities.

Health Sector

The health sector refers to the broad range of services, organizations, and institutions involved in promoting, maintaining, and restoring health. It encompasses various components.

Farming Sector

The farming sector, also known as agriculture, encompasses the cultivation of plants and livestock for food, fiber, and other products used to sustain and enhance human life. It includes a variety of activities and industries.

Education Sector

The education sector encompasses all institutions, organizations, and processes involved in delivering education and training to people of various age groups and skill levels. It includes primary, secondary, and tertiary education, vocational training, professional development, and continuing education programs.

Transport Sector

The transport sector refers to the segment of an economy dedicated to the movement of people, goods, and services from one location to another.

Fishing Sector

The fishing sector, also known as the fisheries sector, encompasses the activities involved in catching, processing, and selling fish and other seafood.
